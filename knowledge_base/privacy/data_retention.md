# Data Retention

## Configurable retention

Customers can configure retention periods for supported data categories within product limits.

## Deletion

Customers can request deletion of customer data; deletion is handled according to the DPA and applicable law, subject to legal hold and backup expiry cycles.

## Security logs

Security logs are retained for a defined operational period to support monitoring, investigation, and compliance.

## Approved wording

Customers can configure retention for supported data categories and request deletion of customer data under our DPA, subject to backup cycles and legal requirements.

## Forbidden wording

Do not promise immediate or instantaneous deletion across all systems and backups.
