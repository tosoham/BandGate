# Data Processing Addendum

## Signing

SentinelAI signs a Data Processing Addendum (DPA) with customers who process personal data through the service.

## Roles and transfers

SentinelAI acts as a processor for customer personal data. Cross-border transfers are governed by the DPA, including Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

## Subprocessors

The DPA references the subprocessors list and customer notice and objection rights.

## Approved wording

We sign a DPA covering processing roles, subprocessors, and cross-border transfer mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable.

## Forbidden wording

Do not commit to a Business Associate Agreement or HIPAA terms without Legal and Compliance approval.
