# Access Control Policy

## Authentication

Multi-factor authentication is enforced for all administrative and privileged access to production systems. Customer-facing access supports SSO.

## Authorization

Access follows least-privilege and role-based access control. Production access is restricted to authorized personnel with a business need and is reviewed periodically.

## Logging

Administrative and privileged actions are logged and monitored.

## Approved wording

We enforce multi-factor authentication for administrative access, apply least-privilege role-based access control, and review production access periodically.

## Forbidden wording

Do not claim that access is impossible to misuse or that no employee can ever access customer data; access is restricted and audited, not absent.
